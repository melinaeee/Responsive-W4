The name says it all. We're 100% women's soccer, 100% of the time. In a world where women's soccer have been perennially underappreciated, underpromoted, and underfunded, WoSo World exists to shine a light on the stories, athletes and moments that define and fuel the women's game. This isn't a token sub-brand or at the bottom of some dropdown menu. We aren't doing this for charity. From jaw-dropping performances to off-court fits, we bring sports fans everything they need to see and know in the world of women's soccer.

Melina Gaspar, creator and founder

Melina is the founder and CEO of WoSo World. Frustrated by a lack of media coverage devoted to women's sports, Melina founded WSW in 2022. Starting with an front end developer bootcamp at SheCodes and a big idea, it took Melina only a few short months to grow WSW into the leading media platform dedicated to women's soccer.
